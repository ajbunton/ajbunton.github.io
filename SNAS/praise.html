Look at all the nice things people have said about us!

Over the years, many people have gone to see SNAS perform. Several of these people even have opinions. Let's take a look back at what some have said.

"They were great! Well I guess it wasn't the best show I ever saw live, but they rocked. Aside from those few miscues, the ocasional mid-song blunder; you know they often did seem to each be playing a different song. There were several hiccups, but man, wow!"
- James Barnraiser, SNAS Enthusiast
"It was better than a swift kick in the nuts."
- Elizabeth Teatotaler, One Time SNAS Groupie
"How did we ever live without SNAS?...Quite happily."
- Frank Futterbottom, Stuck up British Indie Music Journalist
"This is what happens when two Yanks and a Limey come together to make music. May it never happen again."
- Bill Rusterbaws, Stuck up American Indie Music Journalist
"In the early years, Andrew's raging peanut brittle addiction caused high friction with the other band members, almost to the point of self-destruction. His drumming was great, though!"
- Suzie Dix, Peanut Brittle Addiction Specialist
"When I went to see SNAS, I thought I was going to see Styx live in concert. Man I wish I had seen Styx instead."
- Charlie Bucksworth, President "Styx-sux!" Fanclub
"They played a coherant concert....ish."
- Gale Mylastnameisobviouslymadeup, a totally real person, that totally said this.
