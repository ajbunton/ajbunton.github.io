Jul. 21, '09

Postmortem

Dude! A postmortem blog post! Does that mean we're awesome undead rocking zombies!? Wait...what's that? Oh, ok. I've just been informed by our lawyers that no, we are not undead rocking zombies. Damn. I hope the zombies still let us fight on their side when the great zombie uprising occurs.

Anyway, on to buisness [sic]. You may remember that we at one point had a record deal to create an official SNAS album for your listening pleasure, but we were screwed by The Man and lost or deal. Well, we decided to not take things laying down. We decided to put on ninja skillz to use, and take back what was rightfully ours! On a dark and damp night, we snuck into the recording studio, hacked into their mainframe, and took all we could find. We managed to get our hands on 7 (yes 7!) of our songs, which while not enough for a full album, is plenty for an EP. So no we're self-publishing our only EP ever. We're calling it "The Illicit EP." The sound quality isn't real great, but beggars can't be choosers, especially when it comes to questionably obtained sound files.

In further good news, we have some video footage of the Kochi stop on our farewell tour: Melsie Mayhem. We illegally confiscated footage...I mean...had a friend friend film parts of the concert, which we're now making available on DVD, with the help of some of our corporate friends. If you would like a copy of either The Illicit EP on CD or Melsie Mayhem on DVD, send us an email at snas.rock@gmail.com, and we'll get your order in the mail as soon as possible.

Well that's it. Time to go back to retirement. Zomebies, give us a call when the time comes. Stay sexy and rock on, everyone.

Jul. 6, '09

Retirement

The show at Altec was a resounding success! Socks were rocked, ladies swooned, babies were hi-fived, and dudes cried. The greatness of the show, however, is bittersweet, as it shall be SNAS's last. Yes, we're disbanding.

There are several reasons for this. We first noticed a few rifts after Andrew (British) was abducted by pirates for six months. When he finally came back, he reeked of rum and had lost his left hand. We never found out what happened to his hand, but trying to play guitar with a hook for a hand doesn't really work too well. Andrew (Sexy) also suffered a peanut brittle relapse during this time. After months and thousands of dollars in peanut brittle rehab, Andrew fell into dispractice, and never fully regained his drumming chops. While the Andrews were sailing the seven seas and in rehab, Noah hit a bit of a rough patch himself. He finally snapped after spending two years teaching English in Japan. The final straw came when several of his students insisted that watermelon is a vegetable. Noah's brain just couldn't take it, and he suffered a nervous breakdown. Noah still mumbles to himself quite a bit, the only audible words being "watermelon," and "fruit."

Because of all the adversity SNAS has faced recently, we have decided that the show at Altec would be our last. We'd like to thank all our fans for supporting us all this time. We're looking forward to seeing you all on the reunion tour in 2015. [ed. note: 2015 appears to have been a bit optimistic. The reunion tour will actually commence in 2022! Mark your calendars!] So for one last time, rock on and stay sexy!
